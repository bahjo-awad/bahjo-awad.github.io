I'm Bahjo — a First-Class Civil Engineering graduate from Coventry University, now completing a Data Technician Bootcamp that finishes this month.

My background in engineering gave me something most data analysts don't start with: I know what it means when a number is wrong — and what's at stake when decisions are made on bad data. I've applied that rigour to every project I've built.

In the last few months I've built live SQL databases on real-world sales data, published Tableau dashboards on health and employment trends, automated reporting with Python scripts, and designed Power BI reports with DAX-driven KPIs.

I'm looking for a junior data analyst or project controls role where I can bring both technical skill and domain knowledge to a team that cares about getting it right.
